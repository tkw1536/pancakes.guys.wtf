Vegan Pancakes (freedom 🇺🇸 units)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 2 tblsp. of sugar
  • 1/2 teasp. of salt
  • 1 tblsp. of baking powder
  • 5 tblsp. of vanilla sugar
  • 1 cup of non-dairy milk (e.g. Oatly Barista edition)
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries (optional)

Recipe

  • Combine dry ingredients, add wet ingredient, whisk until smooth, let rest for 10 minutes.
  • Bake in non-stick pan. If desired, add blueberries before first flipping.

Notes

  • Makes ≈ 6 pancakes for people
  • For a non-vegan variant, you can use regular milk and add eggs.
  • Not from the US and confused about these units?
  • When vanilla sugar is not available, use either (normal) sugar or (normal) sugar and vanilla extract.
