Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 125 g flour
  • 2 tblsp. of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teasp. of salt
  • 1/2 package of baking powder
  • 1/2 package of vanilla sugar
  • 250 ml of milk
  • 100 g blueberries (optional)

Recipe

  • Combine dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, whisk until smooth, let rest for 10 minutes.
  • Bake in non-stick pan. If desired, add blueberries before first flipping.

Notes

  • Makes ≈ 6 pancakes for people
  • For a vegan variant, replace milk with non-dairy milk and remove eggs.
  • If you're from the US, you can see this in customary units.
  • 1 package of baking powder ≈ 20 ml.
  • 1 package of vanilla sugar ≈ 5 tblsp.
  • When vanilla sugar is not available, use either (normal) sugar or (normal) sugar and vanilla extract.
